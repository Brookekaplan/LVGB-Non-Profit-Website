        <h2>Tutor Training Modules</h2>
        <h3>LVBPT tutors can take advantage of the free online tutor training modules on the ProLiteracy website. This is a great resource! Visit our <a href="https://proliteracyednet.org/lvbpt" target="_blank"> member portal</a> on the Pro Literacy website for more information.</h3>
        <h3>At the bottom of the page, click on "create a tutor account." Then you can create a password and login. Go to the tutor/teacher section of the education network to see a list of the many courses they offer.</h3>
        <h3>There are Basic Literacy and ESOL Tutor Training workshops as well as individual courses on teaching math, writing, vocabulary, phonics, reading comprehension, citizenship and many more topics!</h3>
        <h2>News For You</h2>
        <h3>We have both print and online subscriptions to News For You, which is a newspaper for advanced beginner/intermediate level readers. Weekly issues contain news articles, human interest stories, crossword puzzles, word searches, discussion questions, vocabulary lists, and a teacher's guide. Visit the <a href="https://www.newsforyouonline.com/" target="_blank"> News For You website</a> for more. </h3>

        <h3>On the <a href="https://www.proliteracy.org" target="_blank">Pro Literacy website</a>, you'll find adult literacy statistics and news, as well as free online courses for adult educators.</h3>
        <h2>New Readers Press</h2>
        <h3>New Readers Press is the U.S. publishing division of ProLiteracy Worldwide and the source of many of our adult literacy titles, such as TUTOR and I Speak English. For more information, visit the <a href="https://www.newreaderspress.com" target="_blank"> New Readers Press website</a>, or you may also reach them at 1-800-448-8878.</h3>

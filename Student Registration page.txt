        <h1>Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
        <h2>Where are classes held?</h2>
        <h3>Classes are held at various libraries, churches and community centers in the Bridgeport area.</h3>
        
        <h2>When are classes held?</h2>
        <h3>We have morning, afternoon, evvening and Saturday classes.</h3>
        
        <h2>How do I know which class will be right for me?</h2>
        <h3>When you come in to register at Literacy Volunteers, we will assess your skills and ask about your availability. We'll assign you to a class that is suited to your skill level and your schedule.</h3>

        <h2>How often do classes meet?</h2>
        <h3>Most classes meet once or twice a week for 2 hours.</h3>
        
        <h2>How many students are in a class?</h2>
        <h3>Class sizes generally range from 1 to 8 students. Larger classes may have 2 tutors.</h3>

        <h2>Is there a cost for the classes?</h2>
        <h3>No.</h3>

        <h2>When do classes start and end?</h2>
        <h3>You can register at any time of year to start class. If there is a class that meets your needs, you will join that class. If not, you will be placed on a waiting list and we will contact you as soon as we have a tutor who can work with you.</h3>

        <h2>How long will I be attending class?</h2>
        <h3>We ask that you commit to attending class for at least a year or until you reach your goal (such as passing the GED) but many students decide to continue for several years.</h3>
